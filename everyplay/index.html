What do I do?

Modern agile teams need some one to help them be more effective. Teams need proper tools and methodologies to ease their work and to make better products. Before they know how to test their code, measure the quality and work better as a team, they’re just a bunch of individuals. I’m the guy who can make a bunch of individuals in to a team and great teams in to awesome ones. Teams become more innovative and individuals become the shining stars of your office. I make the workflow smooth and I polish rough edges.

My core knowhow is all kind of testing, continuous deployment and creating new innovative products. If I was to squeeze all my thoughts to one sentence I would say, my true passion is quality.

Developer

I have used bunch of languages and always like to learn more. Already I have used JavaScript, Shell Scripting, Perl, HTML5, CSS3, Python, PHP and C#. Next I want to learn Clojure and want to be better with Perl.

Tester

Testing is one of my favorite things. I love to go for Bug Hunt and try to break things. Even thou I like to find errors and mistakes, more I like to educate developers and testers to become better ones. So I have written testing guidelines for developers and at the moment I am making security testing guidelines.

QA

Some say quality assurance is just standards, rules, restrictions and people shouldn't use them because nowadays we all use agile methodologies. I almost agree with them.

Lets say in team developers decide which kind of high quality code they write, I don't think it is a bad decision or standard. On the other hand rules could educate everybody. For example reading Geotechnical Software Services unit testing guidelines or OWASP Top 10 Most Critical Web Application Security Risks makes you better developer.

DevOps

I love to automate everything. I have made virtual environments to give developers and testers a clone of production. I also have made multiple continuous integration systems with Jenkins.

Enthusiastic contributor

I like to speak in different kind of events and share my knowledge. I have been a speaker in PHP User Group Helsinki and in DevOps Finland.
